Jack Shorter

Lighting Designer and Programmer

Based in Surrey and Hamphsire, Jack Shorter is a theatre practitioner whose primary work involves the design, programming and operation of simple to complex lighting rigs.

Jack has successfully designed and programmed lighting on a variety of productions with especially tight turnarounds for technical departments. He thrives in fast paced, stressful environments.

In April 2022, Jack completed the ABBT Bronze award, achieving a Distinction.

His work extends beyond the design of Lighting Rigs to the operation of consoles for various successful touring shows. He also works as the occasional Sound #2 and maintains the sound system for a local church.
